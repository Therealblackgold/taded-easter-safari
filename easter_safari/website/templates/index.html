Team Taded Schedule

This isn't just any event but an experience of being on safari, at the comfort of a safe venue with beautiful sceneries, camping sites, luxury facilities, entertainment through live music, activities for kids, adults...family fun for all. whithin these three days our guests will be able to fully experience the safari life style and create memories that will last a life time. This event was created to accomidate all ages.

Friday

  • Tents setup & check in from 14:00pm
  • Food will be sold 14:00 pm to 23:00 pm
  • Family Cometitions and Sports from 16:00pm
  • Music Festival 18:00 pm to midnight

Saturday

  • Food will be sold From 07:00am to 23:00pm
  • Excursions 10:00am to 18:00pm
  • Family games 10:00am
  • Zip lining will be open 12:00am
  • Live Music Festival 16:00 to midnight
  • Day care will be open from 13:00pm to 23:00pm

Sunday

  • No program background music, food & drinks as we prepare to leave the farm 12:00pm
